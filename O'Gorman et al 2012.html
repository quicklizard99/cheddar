Paper by O'Gorman et al in Adv. Ecol. Res.

Wed 10 October 2012
by Lawrence Hudson
in news - paper

The authors examined the effects of temperature on the structure of communities in geothermally heated streams. Cheddar was used to create a number of plots and to compute a range of statistics. Body size, biomass and trophic interactions varied greatly with temperature.
