Paper by McLaughlin et al in Adv Ecol Res

Sun 23 February 2014
by Lawrence Hudson
in news - paper

The authors examined how food-web structure varied among a system of naturally fragmented islands in a river network. They used cheddar to produce a number of statistics and figures.

McLaughlin et al (2013) Habitat Isolation Reduces the Temporal Stability of Island Ecosystems in the Face of Flood Disturbance. Advances in Ecological Research, 48 225--284. 10.1016/B978-0-12-417199-2.00004-5
